A brief view of my main programming projects:

  • Extracting competitor keywords

    A project for finding the (organic) keywords competitor websites are promoting. For this purpose I decided to focus on some well known meta tags that are known to be important for Search Engine Optimization (SEO), such as title, meta description, h1, in addition to more sophisticated information like the 10 most common words in the text of a webpage.

    Technologies used: Python, Beautiful Soup

  • Manage expenses

    A project for keeping track, managing and printing our expenses. The outout of this program is a summary of the amount you have spent and the three largest expenses in a given month.

    Technologies used: Java, OOP

  • University projects

    The biggest assignments back from the days when I was a student. These include simulations of a book store, a restaurant, a social network and a system for assigning classrooms to courses.

    Technologies used: Memory handling, Concurrency, Networking, Databases
